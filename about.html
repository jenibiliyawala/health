Our story

We built the advisory firm we wished existed for our own health.

Health Capital started with a simple observation: people manage their retirement accounts with more discipline than their own bodies. We exist to close that gap — with the same rigor, reporting, and long-term thinking you'd expect from a good financial advisor.

Founded 2019

From a single clinic to a full advisory bench

Health Capital opened as a two-person clinic offering annual physicals with unusually thorough reporting. Clients kept asking for the same follow-through their financial advisors gave them — a plan, a review cadence, a number to track. So we built one.

Today the practice is a bench of physicians, dietitians, and behavioral health consultants who work from a single client file, so nothing falls between specialties.

What we believe

Three principles behind every plan

Principle 01

Measure before you manage

No plan is written until we have a real baseline — bloodwork, sleep, movement, and stress, in one file.

Principle 02

Plain-language reporting

Every client gets a quarterly statement written the way we'd want our own results explained to us.

Principle 03

Built to compound

Small, consistent changes tracked over years outperform any single dramatic intervention.
